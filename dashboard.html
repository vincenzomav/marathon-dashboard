Mental Game

Km 1-10: Discipline Phase

"Smooth and patient." You will feel amazing. Everyone does. The discipline is NOT going with the crowd. Run YOUR pace. If people pass you, let them. You'll see them again at km 35.

Km 11-21: Rhythm Phase

"Strong and controlled." Lock into your rhythm. Focus on form: relaxed shoulders, quick feet, arms driving forward. The half-marathon point is NOT halfway — the race starts at km 30.

Km 22-30: Commitment Phase

"I trained for this." The middle is mentally tough because it's neither exciting nor final. Stay process-focused: next km, next aid station, next gel. Break it into 2km chunks.

Km 31-38: Warrior Phase

"One km at a time." This is where the marathon begins. When it hurts, remember every 5AM run, every CrossFit WOD, every long Sunday run. Pain is temporary. This is your day.

Km 39-42.2: Glory Phase

"This is why I'm here." You can see the finish. 3 km is nothing — you've done thousands of them. Smile. Soak it up. You're about to become a sub-3:45 marathoner.
